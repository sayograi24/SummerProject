FAQ

Have questions? We have answers:

Who can donate blood? Generally, healthy adults aged 17-65 can donate blood. Specific eligibility criteria may vary.

How often can I donate? You can donate whole blood every 56 days. Other types of donations may have different intervals.

Is blood donation safe? Yes, donating blood is safe. We use sterile, single-use needles and follow strict health and safety protocols.
